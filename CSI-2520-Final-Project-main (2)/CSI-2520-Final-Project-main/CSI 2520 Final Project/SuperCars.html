Supercars


History


Cars that are labled as a "SuperCar" are also refered to as exotic cars or more recently as hypercars. They are loosely described as street-legal, high-performance luxury sports cars. McLaren, Porshe, LaFerrari, and Mercedes are most commonly recognized as supercars or as hypercars. The price of these cars are usually around the price of around 100,000 dollars and up. They are used as a symbol of wealth and power throughout the world by the rich, famous, and powerful.
